Eligibility Criteria

This account requires an initial deposit of Rs 50,000. The minimum Average Quarterly Balance (AQB) requirement for Assets Start-Up Current Account is Rs 50,000. The date of incorporation / registration of business should not be more than two years before the date of account opening.
The Assets Start-Up Current Account is available for the following:

  • Sole proprietorships
  • Partnerships/limited-liability partnerships
  • Private / public limited companies
  • Educational institutes
  • Insurance / mutual funds
  • Share and stock brokers
